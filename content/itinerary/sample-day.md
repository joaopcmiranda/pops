- **9:00 AM** - Land at Galeão International Airport
- **10:30 AM** - Take taxi/Uber to hotel in Copacabana
- **11:30 AM** - Check in to hotel (if available) or store luggage

- **2:00 PM** - Walk along Copacabana Beach
